EDUCATION CONTACT EBOOK 4.0
ECE4.0 is created, short of Education Contact Ebook, is an application with the automatic attendance by face recognition that is the most highlighted, which works using a mobile device with camera integrated, teachers need to take a photo of all participants faces, the system will automatically recognize and process the attendance results. This application also provides a convenient way of communication and information capture for parent, by updating teachers' posts, monitoring academic transcript, checking attendance or absence, and an online messenger system with teachers
